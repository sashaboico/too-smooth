TooSmooth Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

What TooSmooth does

TooSmooth is a Chrome extension with a popup you open manually. You paste text (an email, DM, or message you want checked) into the popup and click Analyze. That text is sent to the TooSmooth API for analysis, which returns a label (legitimate / human phishing / AI phishing), a risk score, and the manipulation feature scores behind that verdict.

What we collect

Only the text you manually paste into the popup and submit for analysis. Nothing is read, scanned, or transmitted unless you paste text and click Analyze yourself.

What we don't collect

TooSmooth does not read your inbox, Gmail account, or any web page content automatically. It has no access to your browsing activity, email account, or personal identity. It does not run in the background and does not act on any page unless you open the popup and submit text yourself.

Data transmission and retention

Submitted text is sent over HTTPS to the TooSmooth analysis API for the sole purpose of returning a verdict. It is not logged, stored, or retained after the request completes, and it is not shared with any third party.
